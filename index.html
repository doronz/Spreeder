Spreeder

What

A speed reading app for Android that allows you to add your own text and then "Spreed" it. It will flash one word at a time at the chosen WPM (words per minute) setting you choose.

Why

This is a great tool for many reasons:

  • Testing how fast you are able to read by adjusting WPM on the fly.
  • Helps focus because you are forced to pay attention or risk not reading words.
  • Helps you read faster because you do not need to move your eyes to read.

How

Just download the app. Copy an article, essay, or anything you would like to spreed and press the '+' to add it in. When you're ready to start reading hit "Spreed!"

Features

  • Dark and light backgrounds (better theming coming soon)
  • Small, medium and large font sizes (for better experience based on screen size and eye sight)
  • Variable WPM setting (allows long words to remain on the screen slightly longer so that you can process them) -- estimated WPM for this setting coming soon
  • Screen stays on while spreeding
  • Orientation locks while spreeding
  • On the fly adjustable WPM

Planned Features

  • Rewind Sentence
  • Import PDF
  • Share to Spreeder (from other apps)
